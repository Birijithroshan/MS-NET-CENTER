About MS Net Centre

Trusted Service Centre

Your Trusted e-Sevai & Internet Service Centre

MS Net Centre is a fully authorized e-Sevai centre located in the heart of Nochimedu, Manjampatti, Manapparai, Trichy. We are dedicated to providing all types of online government services, document processing, internet services, and digital solutions to the people of our community.

From applying for certificates and government schemes to printouts, passport applications, and Aadhaar services — we handle everything with accuracy and speed. Our goal is to make digital services accessible and hassle-free for everyone.
